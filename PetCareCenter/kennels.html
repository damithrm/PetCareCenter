Kennels and Boarding facility

We offer boarding facilities for your pets both healthy and for those who require veterinary assistance. The kennels and runs are constructed to board all sizes of pets. Your pet will receive specialized treatment such as a nutritious meal, regular exercise and veterinary care when required. Special food and treat supplied by you can be given so that your pet feel at home. Special services can be provided for you to talk to your dog when you are holidaying. Your recorded messages will be played to your pet
