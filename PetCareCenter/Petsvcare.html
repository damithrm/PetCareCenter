In Colombo and its suburbs there is a large population of pets that require basic veterinary care. With PetsVcare ambulatory service a Veterinarian is on call from 8am to 8pm, 7 days a week; and we will provide the owners of the pets a service to their doorstep. Boarding facilities

We offer boarding facilities for your pets both dogs and cats. The kennels and runs are constructed to board all sizes of pets. Your pet will receive specialized treatment such as a nutritious meal, regular exercise and veterinary care when required. Special food and treat supplied by you can be given so that your pet feel at home.
