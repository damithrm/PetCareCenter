Our directors

Mr. Bernard Peiris BSc. (Agri), MSc

Mr. Peiris has his Masters degree in Biochemistry specializing in Animal Nutrition. He held the position of lecturer in animal nutrition at the School of Agriculture Peradeniya and was attached to the Department of Agriculture and held many positions throughout his 18 year career in the Department in the field of animal science.

Dr. Wasantha Kumara BVSc. (Sri Lanka)

Dr. Wasantha completed his degree in the University of Peradeniya in 1986. He completed training programs on Diagnostic avian disease and small animal surgery in Japan and Switzerland respectively.

Dr. Sumith C. Wanniarachchi BVSc. (Sri Lanka)

Dr. Sumith was also graduated from Peradeniya University in 1986. He underwent in a training program on veterinary medicine and surgery in Japan.

Dr. Gamika Prathapasinghe BVSc. (Sri Lanka) PhD. (Canada)

Dr. Gamika completed his degree in 1998 at Peradeniya University and completed his internship at the government veterinary hospital and veterinary teaching hospital Peradeniya. He then joined to the faculty of Medicine, Peradeniya as an Assistant lecturer and at present he works as a lecturer in the University of Wayamba.
